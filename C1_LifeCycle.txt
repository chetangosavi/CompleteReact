lifecycle of Component :
    -> The React component lifecycle refers to the different stages a component goes through from its creation to 
    its removal from the DOM. It consists of three main phases:

    + Phases :
        1. Mounting (Component Creation)
        2. Updating (Re-rendering)
        3. Unmounting (Component Removal)
